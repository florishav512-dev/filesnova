Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners about how users interact with their sites.

This Cookie Policy explains how Files Nova uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential functionality: To ensure our website works properly
  • User experience: To remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our website
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements
  • Security: To protect against malicious activities

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains user session state Session
security_token Prevents cross-site request forgery Session

3.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our website's performance and user experience.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics - session state 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes users 24 hours

3.3 Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to you and your interests. They may also be used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
ad_personalization Personalizes advertisements 30 days
ad_frequency Controls ad frequency 7 days

3.4 Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, more personal features. They may be set by us or by third-party provides whose services we have added to our pages.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
user_preferences Stores user interface preferences 30 days
tool_settings Remembers conversion tool settings 7 days

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services:

4.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website usage and improve our services. Google Analytics sets cookies to help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4.2 Advertising Networks

We may work with third-party advertising networks that use cookies to serve targeted advertisements. These networks have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.

4.3 Content Delivery Networks (CDNs)

We use CDNs to deliver website content efficiently. These services may set their own cookies for performance optimization.

5. How to Control Cookies

5.1 Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from being set
  • Set preferences for specific websites

5.2 Browser-Specific Instructions

Google Chrome

Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data

Mozilla Firefox

Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data

Microsoft Edge

Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

5.3 Opt-Out Tools

You can also use these tools to manage advertising cookies:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out
  • European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance
  • Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, some parts of our website may not function properly. Specifically:

  • Essential cookies: Disabling these may prevent the website from working correctly
  • Functional cookies: You may lose personalized features and preferences
  • Analytics cookies: We won't be able to improve our website based on usage data
  • Advertising cookies: You may see less relevant advertisements

7. Local Storage and Other Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use other technologies such as:

  • Local Storage: To store tool preferences and settings
  • Session Storage: For temporary data during your browsing session
  • Web Beacons: Small transparent images to track email opens and website interactions
  • Fingerprinting: Collecting device and browser information for security purposes

8. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, we may display a cookie consent banner. By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time using your browser settings or by contacting us.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
